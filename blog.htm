Hello World

September 9th 2016

Well here it is! The first iteration of my blog. It's not much, but it's a place where I can put my thoughts. In the future I would like to add more features. The ability for people to comment, tagging, archiving, etc. I’ve considered using Wordpress to handle of these things, no need to reinvent the wheel, and I might still do that. I just don’t have time to really investigate that prospect right now. My most immediate goal is to get this site live a running. So If you’re reading this then, yay go me! Once I’ve got the site live I can start pushing, iterating, and improving things. Here’s to new beginnings!
